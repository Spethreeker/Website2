Data Transfer and Recovery

What we do.

What we do depends on what you want. If you want transfered from one computer to another, we can do that. If you need it retreived from a device, we can probably do that.

Retrieval

If you need data retreived for whatever reason, you will need to supply something to transfer it to or we will sell you a storage device. Obviously, the more data there is to recover, the more storage space you'll need. So, if you want 1 terabyte of data recovered, please supply a 1 terabyte drive.

Transfer

Transfering data pretty much involves the same thing as the retrieval of data, so not much to say here. Just bring something to transfer everything to or we can sell you something.
